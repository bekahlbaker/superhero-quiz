DC and MARVEL CHARACTER QUIZ

You will be presented with the headshots of 39 different DC & Marvel Comic superheroes and villains. Beneath each headshot is a place for you to enter the name of the character. Enter as many as you can and see how you score!
(Remember, this fan tribute is free and just for fun, so don’t get too upset if you can’t get them all - or if one of your favorites was left out.)

  • 1 - 7 : novice
